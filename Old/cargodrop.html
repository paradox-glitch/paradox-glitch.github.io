The cargo drop another of my early models. I created it to experiment with substance painter more.


I also got the chance to use 3DS max’s cloth sim which allowed me to quickly and easily create a realistic net over the supplies.


In substance I got the chance to use alpha masks. Which I created in photoshop.


This allowed me to create text on the supplies.


Again as with my early models I would love to recreate this asset. Applying my newer knowledge. Things I would change is how I unwrapped, how I made the net, and how I used smart materials in substance.
