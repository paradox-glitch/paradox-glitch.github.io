‘Next Ben’ a project created as part of my Next Gen course. The first time id ever touched video composition and VFX, and we had about 3 weeks. The first part of this we concepted ideas, and ended up basing our short on a reddit post. We created worlds in 3ds Max then used unreal to add lighting, I set up camera rigs to record the pans through the scene.


Once exported. We took it in to AE alongside our GreenScreen shots. Using masks and keying we removed ben from the green room and placed him in our world. Next I used the saber plugin to grate the portal effects, and then colour graded everything. Masking again to make things appear as though behind.


Added Credits.


Exported all scenes and put into Pre mire Pro, Added the music from ablation and rendered.


Overall a good project, many mistakes, but for my first time and only in 3 weeks, its good.
