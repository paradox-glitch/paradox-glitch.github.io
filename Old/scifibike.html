The Sci-Fi Bike was one of my first complete modelling and texturing experiences. I created it wanting it to be as futuristic as possible, I aimed to achieve this by making everything as streamlined as possible. I also wanted to create a sense of  minimalism, so in the texturing process I kept it to just a small selection of materials.


This designs inspiration came from a concept pin found on pinterest. I later found out this concept was created for Big Hero 6 by Kevin Nelson.



There a a few errors I made in the creation of my asset. Non of these are to important. I however would love to go back and redesign, model and texture this piece. Especially with all the more advanced knowledge I've learnt over the last 2 year.


For example I would now go create a high and low poly version I could bake for optimisation. I would also make sure to unwrap properly, but before both of these I would  make sure to create better topology.
